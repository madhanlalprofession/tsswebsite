Sringeri App

A devotional app providing information, schedules, and resources related to the Sringeri temple and religious activities.

Project Overview

The Sringeri App is designed to offer devotees access to temple schedules, religious texts, and event updates. It serves as a bridge between the temple administration and devotees worldwide.

Key Features

Temple Schedule

Daily puja timings and special events

Spiritual Resources

Access to religious texts and discourses

Live Streaming

Watch live temple events and sermons

Community Engagement

Participate in discussions and online satsangs
